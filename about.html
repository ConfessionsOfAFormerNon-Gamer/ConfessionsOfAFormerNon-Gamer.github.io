An Introduction

I'm Becky Roesch, a teacher at heart and marketer by trade. My career spans classroom teaching, Apple retail training and customer education. In my current role, I'm a marketer. I help shape our customers' views with my background in education and training. I create clear solutions for complex problems and provide proactive support with creativity. My actions build relationships, and those relationships drive results.

My favorite quote comes from cultural anthropologist Margaret Mead:

"Children must be taught how to think, not what to think."

I focus on meeting learners where they are and inspiring them to go farther. This sentiment, coupled with Mead's directive, guides my approach to learning and customer support. After graduating I'm considering a handful of directions I could take professionally, potentially including:

  • Client onboarding and training, particularly for technology firms
  • Learning experience design for educational platforms
  • Digital literacy education in community settings

Any of these paths would let me put my degree to work in meaningful ways that fuel both personal growth and career satisfaction. Feel free to explore my detailed experience and qualifications in the resumes below.
